Administration & Setup

/setmodroleArgs: roleThe "Master Key" command. Authorizes a specific role to use moderator commands.
/log_setupArgs: channel, eventsConnects the audit logging system to a channel.
/setup_welcomeArgs: channel, messageInitializes the visual welcomer.
/rolepanelArgs: channel, titleCreates a reaction role menu.

Moderation

/banArgs: user, reasonPermanently bans a user.
/kickArgs: user, reasonRemoves a user but allows rejoining.
/muteArgs: user, durationTimes out a user.
/warnArgs: user, reasonIssues a formal warning.
/lockArgs: channelFreezes chat in the channel.
/nukeArgs: noneClones and deletes the current channel (Wipe).

Support Tickets

/setup_ticketArgs: channelDeploys the "Create Ticket" panel.
/claimArgs: noneAssigns the current ticket to you.

Leveling

/rankArgs: userGenerates a visual rank card.
/leaderboardArgs: noneDisplays top 10 users.

Utilities

/remindSets a personal reminder.
/afkSets AFK status.

Fun

/gstartStarts a giveaway.
/suggestPosts a suggestion.
