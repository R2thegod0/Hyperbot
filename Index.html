Administration & Setup

Core infrastructure for Server Owners & Head Admins.

/setmodrole Args: role The "Master Key" command. Authorizes a specific role to use moderator commands.
/log_setup Args: channel, events Connects the audit logging system to a channel to track deleted messages and bans.
/setup_welcome Args: channel, message, image (optional) Initializes the visual welcomer with customizable text and backgrounds.

Moderation

Enforcement tools for Moderators.

/ban Args: user, reason Permanently bans a user from the server.
/tempban Args: user, duration (e.g. 24h), reason Bans a user and automatically unbans them after the set duration.
/lock Args: channel (optional) Freezes chat in the current or specified channel so members cannot type.
/nuke Args: none Clones and deletes the current channel to wipe all history instantly. Irreversible.

Support Tickets

Private 1-on-1 user assistance.

/setup_ticket Args: channel, category Deploys the "Create Ticket" panel to a channel.
/claim Args: none Assigns the current ticket to you, letting other staff know you are handling it.

Utilities

Tools for Power Users.

/remind Args: time, message Sets a personal DM reminder (e.g., /remind 1h Check emails).
/sticky set Args: message Pins a message to the bottom of the chat view, reappearing after every new message.
/whois Args: user Displays deep user analysis including join dates, roles, and account age.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2026

At Horizon Studios, we value your privacy. This policy explains what data Hyper Bot ("we", "us") collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Data We Collect

We collect the minimum amount of data necessary to provide the Bot's core functionality.

A. Automatically Collected Data (Discord API)
When the Bot is in your server, it receives data sent by Discord. This includes: IDs: User IDs, Server IDs, Channel IDs, Role IDs, and Message IDs. Metadata: Timestamps, permissions, and server hierarchy.

B. User-Provided Data (Database Storage)
We store the following data in our secure database to enable features: Configuration: Settings for logs, welcome messages, ticket panels, and auto-roles. Economy & Levels: XP counts, levels, currency balances, and inventory items. Moderation History: Logs of warnings, kicks, bans, and mutes issued via the Bot. Tickets: Transcripts of support tickets (stored as text files attached to log channels, not permanently in our DB). Reminders & Timers: Content of reminders and timers until they expire.

2. Message Content Policy

We do NOT permanently store your message content.
Processing: Message content is processed in RAM (memory) solely for the purpose of Automod filtering, Command handling, and Auto-Responders.
Logging: If you enable "Logging" features, message content (deleted/edited messages) is sent directly to your configured Discord channel. We do not retain a copy of this content on our servers after it is sent to your log channel.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used strictly for: Functionality: To operate features like leveling, moderation, and ticketing. Security: To enforce bans and prevent abuse. Support: To assist you when you contact our support team. Analytics: To track aggregate usage statistics (e.g., "Total Servers") to improve performance.

4. Data Retention

Temporary Data: Cached data (like recent messages for sniping) is cleared every restart.
Persistent Data: Configuration and user stats (XP, Econ) are retained as long as the Bot is in your server.
Deletion: If the Bot leaves a server, configuration data may be deleted from our database after 30 days.

5. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties. Data is only shared with: Discord: As required to function on the platform. Cloud Providers: Trusted infrastructure providers (e.g., VPS hosts) used to run the Bot, who are bound by confidentiality.

6. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

You have the right to: Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you. Rectification: Correct inaccurate data. Erasure: Request deletion of your data ("Right to be Forgotten"). To exercise these rights, please join our Support Server or email horizonstudios.buisness@gmail.com. Please provide your User ID and the specific request.

By using Hyper Bot, you consent to this Privacy Policy.
